Agrikoltura

Agrikoltura is a Maltese organization focused on promoting sustainable agriculture and aquaculture practices. Through community engagement and educational campaigns, Agrikoltura raises awareness about the ecological impacts of fish farming and advocates for responsible practices that protect Malta’s marine biodiversity. Their work includes campaigns to reduce the environmental footprint of fish farming and support alternative, eco-friendly seafood options.
For more information: https://agrikoltura.gov.mt/

Green Deal Malta

Green Deal Malta is an environmental advocacy organization working to address the negative effects of fish farming on Malta’s natural resources. The organization collaborates with local communities and policymakers to encourage sustainable practices in the seafood industry. Green Deal Malta has led initiatives highlighting the issues of water pollution and biodiversity loss linked to fish farming, aiming to influence policy changes that prioritize Malta’s environmental health.
For more information: https://greendeal.mt/
